Project Date Decemeber 10th, 2024
Role Interviewers, Managers
Client Z Star
Visit Zstar-international

During my BBA at the National College of Business Administration and Economics, I teamed up for a project titled "International Interview," analyzing Zstar, a key footwear industry player. By interviewing managers like Adil Manzoor and Miraj Khalid, I explored Zstar’s operations, supply chain, and competitive strategies, gaining insights into balancing innovation and workforce well-being. This project highlights my research and analytical skills, making it a strong addition to my portfolio..

Crafting this report sharpened my teamwork, critical thinking, and presentation skills. We analyzed Zstar’s 50/50 China-Pakistan supplier split, seasonal production adjustments, and product innovation to beat competitors, alongside their government and public interactions. This project showcases my ability to dissect complex business structures and deliver clear insights, enhancing my portfolio for business analysis and management roles.
